The Firm

Divorce Financial Planning, LLC is a fee-only financial planning practice for men and women of all ages. As a fee-only firm, we charge only for the use of our time for our compensation. We do not collect commissions on any products we recommend, such as insurance or investments, nor referral fees from any professionals we recommend, including: attorneys, insurance agents, therapists, or other specific professionals.

The most important feature of Divorce Financial Planning, LLC is that we listen to your concerns — all of them — and work hard to determine your financial priorities and requirements, both now and especially for the future.

Here are some examples of questions asked by our clients who are anticipating or going through a divorce:

  • “Will I have enough money after my divorce?”
  • “Will I be able to get health care insurance?”
  • “Will I be able to retire comfortably?”
  • “Will there be money for my children’s college education?”

We help answer these questions and many more by first evaluating your current lifestyle. This includes an in depth analysis of your income, assets, liabilities and ultimate goals and dreams. One of our strengths is the use of specialized, sophisticated financial software to help determine the reasonability of financial asset division proposals made during the divorce negotiations for both the immediate and long term affects on both parties.
