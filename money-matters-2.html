The Time Value of Money and Its Importance to You

To put it frankly, money matters! At Divorce Financial Planning, LLC, located in the Charlottesville, Va., region, we specialize in providing expert financial services to individuals and couples who are contemplating divorce, planning to divorce or in the midst of divorce negotiations. As financial planning advisors, we’re available for all stages of the separation and divorce process — including the pre-decision, pre-filing, post-filing and post-divorce portion of your life.

What Divorce Financial Planning, LLC does not do is provide psychological or legal advice. We can recommend professionals in those fields, but the final choice of representation or help is a client’s responsibility alone. We also refer clients who are considering divorce to marriage counselors if the final decision has not yet been reached.

The cost of divorce — both financial and emotional — is daunting. Most married people recognize this, but the overall cost of many divorces is even worse than couples imagined before they begin the process.

The old saying “time is money” is never more appropriate than in a divorce. However, there is a fine line between settling for less than you deserve and insisting on more than you can reasonably expect to receive. That mid-point line is the optimal point of settlement. Our goal at Divorce Financial Planning, LLC is to zero in on that fine line. The more of a couple’s assets that are left to divide after the divorce process, the better prepared both parties are in starting the next phase of their lives.
